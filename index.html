How it works

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does RenovateIQ Business cost? +
RenovateIQ Business costs £9.99/$12.99 per month per contractor. Clients and subcontractors join your project for free using an invite code — they never pay anything.
Do my clients need to pay? +
No. Clients and subcontractors join your project for free using a simple 8-character invite code. Only the contractor pays the monthly subscription.
What devices does RenovateIQ Business work on? +
RenovateIQ Business is available on iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
Can I manage multiple projects at once? +
Yes. Your subscription gives you unlimited projects, unlimited clients and unlimited subcontractors across all your active jobs.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store. No contracts, no cancellation fees.
What's the difference between a client and a subcontractor in the app? +
Clients see project progress, updates and photos — keeping them informed without being overwhelmed. Subcontractors see their assigned tasks and rooms and can message the contractor directly.
